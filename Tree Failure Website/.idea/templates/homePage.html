Understanding Tree Failure & Risk Assessment

Analyzing the factors that contribute to tree failure to promote safety and environmental awareness.

About Tree Failure Analysis

Tree failure analysis examines the structural integrity of trees and the external factors leading to collapse. By studying past incidents, we can predict potential failures and take preventive measures.

Key Risk Factors

  • Tree species and age
  • Environmental conditions
  • Soil stability
  • Previous damage or decay
